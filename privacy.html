Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2026

Welcome to Harvion Labs. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our AI platform, applications, and services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the most advanced AI experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and authentication credentials when you sign up or contact us.
  • AI Interaction Data: The text prompts, voice inputs, and files you submit to the Harvion AI engine (including the "Jugaad Engine" and "Roast & Boost" features).
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, device information, and usage metrics to ensure platform stability.

2. How We Use Your Data

We do not collect data just for the sake of it. Your information is used strictly to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Harvion AI ecosystem.
  • Process your prompts and generate accurate, context-aware responses.
  • Improve our AI models (Note: We use anonymized and aggregated data for training).
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activities or abuse of the AI system.

3. Data Sharing & Third-Party Partners

We do NOT sell your personal data to data brokers or advertising agencies. However, to power our core intelligence, we may securely share necessary data with:

  • Core API Providers: Essential processing partners who process prompts strictly to generate responses.
  • Cloud Hosting: Secure server providers (e.g., VerceL) who host our infrastructure.
  • Legal Compliance: Law enforcement, if required by a valid legal subpoena or to protect our platform's integrity.

4. AI Model Training & Opt-Out

Harvion Labs believes in user control. While we use generalized data to make our AI smarter and more culturally aware, users on our Pro and Enterprise plans are automatically opted out of having their specific inputs used for future model training. Free tier users can request an opt-out via our privacy team.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS for data in transit. However, no electronic transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please do not share highly sensitive personal information (like passwords or bank details) directly with the AI.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (including GDPR and CCPA compliances), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the deletion of your account and chat history.
  • Opt-out of certain data processing activities.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact our Executive Privacy Desk at:

Email: privacy@harvionlabs.space
